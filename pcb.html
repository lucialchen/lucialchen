OVERVIEW

I really enjoyed the open-ended design component of the project. I first attempted to route the components of my circuit in a neat and condensed way, so as to frame the shape of my PCB around a visually pleasing shape. Once I completed my trace paths, I decided I wanted to incorporate the LED light component into the shape. Thus, I came up with an abstract star outline for my PCB, and I used a yellow LED when soldering to further complement the theme of my project.

Reflection

Many parts of this project were more difficult than I imagined, which made completing each step feel more rewarding. I didn’t quite understand how to efficiently draw the trace paths at first, resulting in some unnecessary traces before I realized that components could be electrically connected through chains of connections. The soldering was a labor of fixing shorts and overusing the desoldering tool. In the end, I had a blinking star shaped PCB!
